Session Format

  • Duration 50 minutes per session
  • Frequency Weekly sessions
  • Format Video conference (online only)
  • Languages English and Italian

The psychodynamic informed approach takes place during weekly 50-minute video conference sessions. This consistent framework supports the depth of exploration this work requires.

Fees

Fees are available on enquiry.

I am prepared to offer a 15-minute video call at no charge prior to undertaking the assessment to allow for any questions about this approach, my practice, or the suitability of this treatment for your circumstances.
