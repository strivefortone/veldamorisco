Languages

Therapeutic Orientation

My practice is grounded in psychodynamic psychotherapy, an approach that focuses on understanding how past experiences and unconscious patterns influence current emotions, relationships, and ways of relating.

The focus of this therapeutic work involves exploring these patterns and understanding how they may shape behaviours and current relationships, aiming at greater self-awareness and emotional insight.
